Question 1:  I can’t write a new article. What do I need to do?

Answer: If you haven’t registered an account yet, you can do so by clicking on ‘I’m new here’ when the login form is visible. You can also do this by going to newstopia.pythonanywhere.com/account/register. After you registered your account, you can login by clicking the buddy icon (‘login’). If you click on the pencil icon in the menu (‘create’) now, you will be able to write an article.

Question 2:  What are the numbers displayed in each article?

Answer: This is the article’s rating. If you are logged in, you will be able to upvote or downvote every article once, using the thumbs up or down icons. When you are not logged in, you will only see the rating and you won’t be able to change it.

Question 3:   Can I search by tag?

Answer: Yes. If you put a ‘#’ in front of your search request, it will look for articles that have that keyword as a tag

Question 4:   I can’t write a new paragraph for an article. What do I need to do?

Answer: See 2. Additionally, you will have to click on ‘detail’ of an article on the front page once logged in to write a new paragraph.

Question 5:   What are the numbers displayed below each paragraph when I click on ‘detail’ in an article?

Answer: See 4.

Question 6:   Is there a profile page?

Answer: Yes. Once you are logged in, the buddy icon’s text will change to profile. If you click the icon, you will be transfered to your profile page. In addition, once logged in the buddy icon will display the first letter of your email address.

Question 7:   Which paragraphs are displayed in an article?

Answer: The 5 highest paragraphs are displayed.

Question 8:   How do I use the search bar?

Answer: If you click on the ‘?’ icon, the search bar will drop down from the top of the screen. Here, you can input characters to search for. The search will start when you press enter.

Question 9:   What do the stars on my profile page mean?

Answer: They’re an indication of how you compare to the other members. If you have 5 stars in a category, it means you are in the top 20% for that category.
